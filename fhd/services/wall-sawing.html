Precision Track-Mounted Wall Sawing

Wall Sawing is the most effective method for creating precise cuts in vertical or steeply inclined surfaces. At Fajr Al Dhaid, we use advanced track-mounted diamond saws that can cut through heavily reinforced concrete with ease, ensuring straight, clean edges every time.

Common Applications

  • Cutting openings for doors, windows, and ventilation.
  • Structural modifications to existing buildings.
  • Precise demolition of wall sections.
  • Cutting of concrete retaining walls.

Why Wall Sawing?

Unlike traditional demolition methods, wall sawing is vibration-free and extremely precise. This prevents micro-cracks in the remaining structure and eliminates the need for extensive patching or finishing work. It is the ideal solution for renovation projects where structural integrity is paramount.
